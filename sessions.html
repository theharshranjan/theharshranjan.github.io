Questions

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before booking

Do I need prior experience in Yoga or Ayurveda?

Not at all! We welcome complete beginners. Sessions are tailored to your current level and we build gradually from where you are.

Is this medical treatment or therapy?

No. These sessions are educational and wellness-oriented. For medical concerns, please consult a licensed physician. For mental health treatment, please see a licensed therapist.

How does the donation-based pricing work?

You contribute what feels right for you after the session. We accept payments via UPI, PayPal, bank transfer, or other methods that work for your location. The focus is on accessibility, not profit.

What time zones do you accommodate?

All time zones are welcome! We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate clients worldwide. Book a time that works best for you during the booking process.

Will I receive materials after the session?

Yes! You'll receive a concise practice outline with notes, recommended resources, and guidance for continuing your practice independently.

What if English isn't my first language?

Sessions are conducted with patience and clarity. We use simple language, visual demonstrations, and ensure you understand every step. Don't worry about perfect English!
