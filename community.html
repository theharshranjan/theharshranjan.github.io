The Process

How Members Are Welcomed

01

Application

Practitioner applies via email with academic credentials, area of practice, and a brief statement of intent.

02

Review

Harsh Ranjan personally reviews each application. Members are selected for authenticity, dedication, and alignment with Samarpan's values.

03

Profile Creation

A dedicated profile page is crafted for every accepted member — capturing their journey, credentials, honours, and unique member ID.

04

Welcome to the Circle

Member receives their unique Samarpan ID, a shareable profile URL, and becomes part of the growing Samarpan wellness network.
