What is Samarpan

A Sanctuary Built
on Complete Surrender

The word Samarpan means complete surrender — the courageous act of releasing what no longer serves so that real healing can begin. It is not defeat; it is the deepest form of trust.

Founded in New Delhi in 2020, Samarpan is a holistic wellbeing community that bridges five thousand years of Vedic science with modern psychology and neuroscience. We serve individuals across India and globally — online and in person.

We are not a clinic. We are not a spa. We are a living community of practitioners and seekers, united by the conviction that true healing is multidimensional — body, mind, breath, and soul.

Our Approach

The Three Pillars of Samarpan

No single science holds the complete answer. True healing lives at the intersection.

01
Pillar One

Ayurveda

The science of life. Ayurveda teaches us that each person has a unique constitution — their Prakriti — and that health means living in harmony with it. We use Dosha assessment, Panchakarma, herbal protocols, and seasonal living to restore the body's natural intelligence.

Explore Ayurveda →
02
Pillar Two

Yoga & Meditation

Beyond postures, Yoga is a complete science of consciousness. Our practice draws from the Bihar School of Yoga tradition — Hatha, Pranayama, Yoga Nidra, Ajapa Japa, and deep meditation — to transform the pranic body and dissolve the roots of suffering.

Explore Yoga →
03
Pillar Three

Psychology

The mind holds what the body carries. We integrate somatic trauma therapy, depth psychology, and transpersonal approaches — grounded in both modern neuroscience and ancient Vedic understanding — to heal the patterns that ordinary therapy alone cannot reach.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

01

Satya — Truth

Honest, evidence-informed practice. We never overpromise. Healing takes time, and we honour that truth with every person we serve.

02

Seva — Service

Every session is an act of sacred service. Our practitioners serve seekers from a place of genuine care — not transaction.

03

Samas — Integration

Body, mind, and soul are held together. No dimension of a person is left outside the healing space. Wholeness is both the goal and the method.

04

Shakti — Empowerment

We do not create dependency. The goal is to awaken each person's own inner intelligence and their lasting capacity for self-healing.

Our Heritage

Rooted in Living Tradition

Bihar School of Yoga

Our Yoga practice is rooted in the tradition of Swami Satyananda Saraswati — one of the most rigorous and complete Yoga lineages in the world, transmitted through the Bihar School of Yoga, Munger.

Rikhiapeeth Lineage

Our founder is initiated by Swami Satyasangananda Saraswati (Swami Satsangi), Peethadhishwari of Rikhiapeeth — a living ashram dedicated to selfless service and Tantric Yoga transmission.

Ayurveda — JNU & Classical India

Grounded in both living tradition and academic rigour — formal study in Ayurveda Biology at Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi, alongside practice drawn from classical Sanskrit texts.
