The Founder

Harsh Ranjan

True wellness is not compartmentalized. It does not separate the mind from the body, or the biological from the spiritual.

My journey focuses on the profound intersection of Ayurveda Biology and the transformative power of Yoga. By understanding our biological constitution (Prakriti), we unlock the code to our mental and spiritual potential.

At Samarpan, I offer a path that is deeply rooted in the Vedic tradition yet rigorously validated by modern physiological understanding.

The Two Pillars of Samarpan

Ayurveda Biology

Ayurveda is not merely about herbal remedies; it is the science of life (*Ayu* + *Veda*). My approach, grounded in a BSc in Ayurveda Biology, views the body as a dynamic system of genetic expression and metabolic fire (*Agni*).

  • Prakriti Analysis: Understanding your unique biological blueprint (Vata, Pitta, Kapha) to prevent disease before it manifests.
  • Dinacharya (Bio-Rhythms): Aligning your daily routine with circadian biology to optimize hormonal balance and gene expression.
  • Ahara (Nutrition): Using food as molecular information to heal the gut-brain axis and restore immunity (Ojas).

Yoga

Yoga is the technology of consciousness. Through traditional practice and the ancient wisdom of the Yoga Sutras, we move beyond physical exercise into the realm of somatic healing and spiritual awakening.

  • Somatic Release: Using Asana not just for flexibility, but to discharge trauma stored in the nervous system via mechanotransduction.
  • Pranayama: Regulating the Vagus Nerve through breath to shift from fight-or-flight to deep restoration.
  • Dhyana (Meditation): Systematic techniques to refine the mind, improve focus, and expand awareness.

"We are not biological machines trying to be spiritual. We are spiritual beings operating through a biological machine. To heal one, we must understand the other."
