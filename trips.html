Recent Yatras

🕉️ Rishikesh

How to Reach: Nearest airport is Dehradun (Jolly Grant, 20 km). Connected by trains and buses from Delhi & Haridwar.
What to Visit: Triveni Ghat Ganga Aarti, Parmarth Niketan, Swarg Ashram, Ram Jhula, Laxman Jhula, and meditation by the Ganga.

🌊 Haridwar

How to Reach: Well connected by train from Delhi, Dehradun, and Varanasi.
What to Visit: Har ki Pauri Ganga Aarti, Mansa Devi Temple, Chandi Devi Temple, Bharat Mata Mandir, and Daksha Mahadev Temple at Kankhal.

🕉️ Sri Kashi Vishwanath, Varanasi

How to Reach: Nearest airport: Lal Bahadur Shastri Airport (Varanasi). Well connected by trains.
What to Visit: Sri Kashi Vishwanath Jyotirlinga, Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat, Sankat Mochan Hanuman Temple, and Sarnath.

🙏 Baba Baidhyanath, Deoghar

How to Reach: Nearest railway station: Jasidih Junction (7 km). Patna & Ranchi airports are nearby.
What to Visit: Baba Baidhyanath Jyotirlinga Temple, Basukinath Temple, Tapovan Caves, and Trikuta Parvat.

🕉️ Mahakaleshwar, Ujjain

How to Reach: Ujjain is well connected via Indore Airport (55 km) and train routes.
What to Visit: Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga Bhasma Aarti, Kal Bhairav Temple, Ram Ghat, Sandipani Ashram, and Harsiddhi Shaktipeeth.

🕉️ Omkareshwar

How to Reach: 80 km from Indore, connected by road & train (Omkareshwar Road Station).
What to Visit: Omkareshwar Jyotirlinga Temple (island shaped like ॐ), Mamleshwar Temple, Parikrama of Mandhata Hill, and serene Narmada ghats.

🌸 Vindhyachal Shaktipeeth

How to Reach: 70 km from Varanasi, connected by rail (Vindhyachal Station) and road.
What to Visit: Vindhyavasini Devi Temple, Ashtabhuja Temple, Kali Khoh, and the sacred Trikona Parikrama.
