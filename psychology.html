Deep Psychology

The Science of Samskaras

In Yoga philosophy, Samskaras are the deep mental grooves or impressions left by past actions. In Neurobiology, we call this Long-Term Potentiation—"neurons that fire together, wire together."

My therapeutic approach focuses on identifying these maladaptive loops. We don't just "talk" about them; we interrupt the neural signal using Pranayama (breathwork) to shift the autonomic nervous system from 'Fight or Flight' to 'Rest and Digest', allowing new, healthier pathways to form.

"We are not victims of our biology; we are the architects of our neural pathways."
