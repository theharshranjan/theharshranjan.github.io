Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our counseling sessions

Is this medical or psychiatric treatment?

No. This is educational, wellness-focused counseling and coaching. It is not a substitute for licensed medical or psychiatric care. If you're experiencing severe mental health challenges, I recommend consulting with a licensed mental health professional.

Can I attend sessions from outside India?

Absolutely! Sessions are conducted online and open to participants worldwide. We'll coordinate time zones to find a suitable slot that works for both of us.

What techniques and tools might we use?

Our work together may include journaling prompts, cognitive reframing techniques, breathwork practices, mindfulness exercises, habit scaffolding, gentle lifestyle alignment with Ayurvedic principles, and personalized meditation practices.

How is my privacy protected?

Your information is handled with strict confidentiality within the limits of the law and the secure platforms used for scheduling and video calls. I respect your privacy and create a safe, judgment-free space for our work together.

What if I'm experiencing a mental health crisis?

If you're in immediate danger or experiencing a mental health emergency, please contact emergency services right away. In India, dial 112 for emergencies or 1800-599-0019 (KIRAN) for 24√ó7 mental health support. Outside India, contact your local emergency services or mental health crisis line.

How many sessions will I need?

This varies by individual and goals. Some clients find value in a single session for specific guidance, while others prefer ongoing support. We can discuss what feels right for you during our first session, with no pressure for commitment.

Do you offer sessions in languages other than English?

Sessions are primarily conducted in English and Hindi. If you need support in another language, please mention this in your booking form and we'll explore possibilities.
