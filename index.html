About Me

Bridging Ancient Wisdom with Modern Science

I'm Harsh Ranjan (Samarpan) — an interdisciplinary scholar-practitioner specializing in Ayurveda Biology with parallel studies in Psychology, Yoga, and Sanskrit. My mission is to bridge the profound wisdom of ancient healing traditions with the precision of modern scientific research, making holistic wellness accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Through Ārogya Peetham – Center for Integrative Health, I serve a diverse global community spanning India, USA, Singapore, Netherlands, UK, Canada, and Australia. Whether you're managing stress in New York, seeking balance in Singapore, or exploring wellness in Mumbai, I provide personalized integrative health programs that respect your cultural context and lifestyle.

Ayurveda

Ancient Science of Life - Balancing Body, Mind & Spirit

Ayurveda, the 5,000-year-old healing science from India, offers timeless wisdom for modern wellness. It emphasizes balance between the three doshas and harmony between body, mind, and spirit. Through personalized dietary recommendations, therapeutic practices, and lifestyle guidance, Ayurveda helps you unlock your natural potential and achieve lasting vitality. Discover natural therapies, daily wellness routines, and timeless wisdom that nurtures inner harmony.
