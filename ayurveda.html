आयुर्वेद

Apaurusheya Vidya

The Science
of Life

“

Ayurveda is not merely a system of medicine; it is the art of living in harmony with the laws of nature. It teaches us that we are a microcosm of the universe.

”
Charaka Samhita, Sutrasthana 1.41

हिताहितं सुखं दुःखं आयुस्तस्य हिताहितम् ।
मानं च तच्च यत्रोक्तं आयुर्वेदः स उच्यते ॥

(Hitāhitaṃ sukhaṃ duḥkhaṃ āyustasya hitāhitam |
Mānaṃ ca tacca yatroktaṃ āyurvedaḥ sa ucyate ||)

"Ayurveda is that which deals with good, bad, happy, and unhappy life, its promoters and non-promoters, measure and nature."

The Goal: Swasthya

Health in Ayurveda is not just the absence of disease. It is a state of spiritual, mental, and physiological radiance. As defined by Sushruta:

समदोषः समाग्निश्च समधातुमलक्रियः ।
प्रसन्नात्मेन्द्रियमनाः स्वस्थ इत्यभिधीयते ॥

"One whose Doshas (humors), Agni (digestive fire), Dhatus (tissues), and Malas (wastes) are balanced, and whose Soul, Senses, and Mind are pleasant, is considered Healthy."

Prakriti Analysis

The Tridosha Theory

Everything in the universe is composed of the five elements (Pancha Mahabhuta). In the human body, these combine to form three bio-energies.

Vata

Air + Ether

तत्र रूक्षो लघुः शीतः खरः सूक्ष्मश्चलोऽनिलः ।

The energy of movement. Governs breathing, pulsation, muscle movement, and nerve impulses.

  • Nature: Dry, Light, Cold, Mobile
  • Site: Colon, Thighs, Bones
  • Imbalance: Anxiety, Insomnia, Pain

Pitta

Fire + Water

पित्तं सस्नेह तीक्ष्णोष्णं लघु विस्रं सरं द्रवम् ।

The energy of transformation. Governs digestion, metabolism, temperature, and intelligence.

  • Nature: Hot, Sharp, Light, Oily
  • Site: Small Intestine, Blood, Eyes
  • Imbalance: Inflammation, Anger, Acidity

Kapha

Earth + Water

स्निग्धः शीतो गुरुर्मन्दः श्लक्ष्णो मृत्स्नः स्थिरः कफः ।

The energy of lubrication and structure. Governs stability, immunity, growth, and lubrication.

  • Nature: Heavy, Cold, Stable, Unctuous
  • Site: Chest, Stomach, Joints
  • Imbalance: Lethargy, Congestion, Weight
