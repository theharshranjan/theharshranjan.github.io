Nutrition Myths Debunked

Separating fact from fiction with evidence

Carbs make you fat

Fact: Excess calories cause weight gain, not carbs themselves. Focus on quality: prefer whole grains and avoid refined, sugar-dense carbs.

Fat is always bad

Fact: Healthy fats (nuts, seeds, vegetable oils rich in unsaturated fats) are essential. Limit saturated & trans fats as advised by WHO.

Supplements replace food

Fact: While supplements can help when medically indicated, whole foods provide fiber, phytochemicals, and balanced nutrients. Always follow professional guidance.

Detox diets are necessary

Fact: Your liver and kidneys detoxify daily. Support them with hydration, fiber, and balanced meals â€” restrictive cleanses aren't necessary.

Ayurveda Notes

Practical Ayurvedic pointers focused on digestion

  • Eat warm & cooked: Easier digestion; prefer warm fluids if digestion is weak
  • Digestive spices: Cumin, coriander, fennel, and ginger aid digestion in small amounts
  • Meal timing: Finish main meal when the sun is relatively high; avoid heavy late-night meals frequently
  • Dosha-aware tweaks: (Vata) warm soups; (Pitta) cooling foods and less spice; (Kapha) lighter meals and more greens
