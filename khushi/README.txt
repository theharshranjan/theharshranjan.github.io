FOR KHUSHI — HOW TO ADD YOUR OWN PHOTOS, VIDEO & MUSIC
========================================================

Everything is wired up already. You just need to drop your files into
the right folders using the exact names below (or open the .html files
and change the file names to match your own — search for "EDIT ME").

1) MUSIC (plays via the note button, bottom-right, on every page)
   -> Put one MP3 file here:  assets/audio/theme.mp3
   -> That's it — every page will pick it up automatically.

2) HOME PAGE TEASER VIDEO
   -> Put one MP4 file here:  assets/video/teaser.mp4

3) OUR STORY — timeline photos (5 of them)
   -> assets/img/story-01.jpg   (2013 entry)
   -> assets/img/story-02.jpg   (2016 entry)
   -> assets/img/story-03.jpg   (2019 entry)
   -> assets/img/story-04.jpg   (2022 entry)
   -> assets/img/story-05.jpg   (2026 entry)
   -> Also open our-story.html and edit the years/titles/text to your
      real story (look for "EDIT ME").

4) GALLERY — 9 photos + 1 video
   -> assets/img/gallery-01.jpg  through  assets/img/gallery-09.jpg
   -> assets/video/gallery-video.mp4
   -> Until you add a file, that tile just shows a soft placeholder —
      nothing looks broken.

5) THE LETTER
   -> Open letter.html and replace the paragraphs inside
      <div class="letter-body"> with your real words.

6) THE MOMENT (proposal page)
   -> Open proposal.html and edit the headline, the sub-line, and the
      question text (search for "EDIT ME").

TIPS
----
- Photos: JPG or PNG, ideally under 500KB each so pages load fast.
- Video: MP4 (H.264), keep clips short (10-30 sec) for a quick load.
- Music: MP3, keep it under ~5MB if you can.
- If a browser blocks autoplay of music, that's normal — the visitor
  just needs to click anywhere on the page once, or tap the music
  button directly.
- To preview locally, just double-click index.html to open it in your
  browser. To publish, upload the whole "for-khushi" folder (keeping
  the assets subfolders) to GitHub Pages, Netlify, or your usual host.
