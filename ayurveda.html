What is Ayurveda Biology?

Traditional Ayurveda is often misunderstood as just "herbal medicine" or "massage." Ayurveda Biology is the scientific integration of these ancient principles with modern systems biology.

By studying the molecular basis of Tridosha (Vata, Pitta, Kapha), we can understand how your unique phenotype influences your gut health, neural pathways, and susceptibility to disease. This is personalized medicine in its purest form.

The Bio-Energies

Understanding Your Prakriti

Vata

Air + Ether

The energy of movement. Governs the nervous system, breathing, and creativity. When imbalanced, causes anxiety, bloating, and dryness.

Signs of Balance: Creativity, flexibility, quick learning.
Signs of Imbalance: Fear, insomnia, constipation.

Pitta

Fire + Water

The energy of transformation. Governs metabolism, digestion (Agni), and intelligence. When imbalanced, causes anger, inflammation, and acidity.

Signs of Balance: Intelligence, strong digestion, leadership.
Signs of Imbalance: Anger, ulcers, skin rashes.

Kapha

Earth + Water

The energy of structure. Governs stability, lubrication, and immunity (Ojas). When imbalanced, causes lethargy, weight gain, and depression.

Signs of Balance: Calmness, strength, endurance.
Signs of Imbalance: Greed, attachment, congestion.
