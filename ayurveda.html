The Science of Life

Ayurveda Biology

Bridging the ancient Vedic understanding of Tridosha with modern biological concepts of genetics, epigenetics, and metabolism.

Prakriti & Genetics

Your Prakriti (constitution) is fixed at conception, much like your DNA. In Ayurveda Biology, we analyze your phenotypic traits to understand your genetic predispositions towards certain imbalances.

Agni & Metabolism

Health is not just what you eat; it is what you digest. We focus on optimizing *Jatharagni* (digestive fire) and cellular metabolism to prevent the formation of *Ama* (metabolic toxins).

Dinacharya & Rhythms

Aligning with the body's circadian rhythms is crucial for gene expression. We create personalized daily routines that respect the biological clock of your organs.

The Tridosha Theory

Vata

Ether + Air

Governs all movement, the nervous system, and elimination. When balanced: Creativity. When aggravated: Anxiety, bloating.

Pitta

Fire + Water

Governs metabolism, digestion, and intelligence. When balanced: Focus. When aggravated: Inflammation, anger.

Kapha

Earth + Water

Governs structure, lubrication, and immunity. When balanced: Endurance. When aggravated: Lethargy, congestion.
