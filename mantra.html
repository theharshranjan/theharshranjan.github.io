Gayatri Mantra

Illumination of Intellect

ॐ भूर्भुवः स्वः
तत्सवितुर्वरेण्यं
भर्गो देवस्य धीमहि
धियो यो नः प्रचोदयात् ॥

"Om Bhur Bhuva Swaha, Tat Savitur Varenyam, Bhargo Devasya Dheemahi, Dhiyo Yo Nah Prachodayat"

Benefit: Awakens the Ajna Chakra (Wisdom center). Recommended for students, professionals, and seekers of clarity.

Healing & Protection

"Om Tryambakam Yajamahe, Sugandhim Pushti Vardhanam, Urvarukamiva Bandhanan, Mrityor Mukshiya Maamritat"

Benefit: The great death-conquering mantra. Used for physical healing, overcoming fear, and vitality (Ojas).
