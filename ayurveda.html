A systems view of health—rooted in classics, tested by science

From Prakriti and Agni to breath, posture, and meditation—an integrated approach you can practice every day.

1) Foundations

Prakriti, Tridoṣa balance, Agni, Dhātu and Srotas as the explanatory map. Assessment emphasizes digestion, sleep, stress load, and seasonal context.

2) Integration with Yoga

Yoga drives regulation: breath (prāṇāyāma), posture (āsana), relaxation (nidra), mantra & meditation—sequenced by dosha and time-of-day.

Asana → regulatePrāṇāyāma → balanceMeditation → clarity

3) Evidence-Informed Practice

Classical rationale + modern outcomes. We use available trials and reviews to shape safe, scalable routines while avoiding over-claims.

Personal routine (demo) — choose your predominant dosha

Morning

  • Warm water; gentle abhyanga (sesame).
  • Asana: slow, grounding (forward bends, hip-openers).
  • Prāṇāyāma: Nāḍi Śodhana 6–10 mins.

Day & Meals

  • Regular warm meals; avoid skipping.
  • Spices: ginger, cumin, ajwain.
  • Short sun-walks; reduce overstimulation.

Evening

  • Yoga Nidra 15–20 mins.
  • Golden milk or warm herbal tea.
  • Consistent lights-out time.

Morning
