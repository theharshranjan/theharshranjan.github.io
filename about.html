Philosophy & Journey

Where Ancient Science Meets Modern Rigor

My path is a synthesis of the laboratory and the spiritual heart.

Having completed my BSc in Ayurveda Biology at JNU and currently pursuing BA Hons in Psychology at IGNOU, my academic journey is fueled by a desire to validate ancient wisdom through scientific inquiry. This bridge allows me to understand human health through the lenses of Neurobiology, genetics, and cognitive science.

Simultaneously, I have immersed myself in the classical roots of these traditions. Mastering Sanskrit at the Central Sanskrit University (Certificate 2024, Diploma 2025) has allowed me to translate Mantra and clinical texts without dilution. This is coupled with advanced yogic training at the Bihar School of Yoga.

The Integrated Approach

Every session at Samarpan is an intersection of institutional research and traditional initiation, designed to return you to your natural constitution.
