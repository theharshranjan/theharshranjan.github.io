Philosophy & Journey

Harsh Ranjan is a holistic wellbeing practitioner dedicated to bridging the profound gap between ancient Vedic wisdom and modern scientific understanding. With a diverse academic background spanning Ayurveda Biology, Psychology, Yoga and Neurobiology, Harsh provides a multidimensional approach to health that addresses the body, the mind, and the underlying consciousness.

His journey is a synthesis of the laboratory and the spiritual heart. By validating ancient practices through the lens of modern rigor, every session at Samarpan is designed to return you to your original biological and spiritual constitution.
