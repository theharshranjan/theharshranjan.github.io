1. Overview

Samarpan ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you visit our website at thesamarpan.co.in, use our services, or interact with us in any way.

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of our site. By accessing or using our Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

Plain English Summary: We collect only what we need to serve you well. We never sell your data. You can ask us to delete it any time.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Submit a contact or booking form (name, email, phone, message)
  • Subscribe to our newsletter (email address)
  • Register for a free yoga session or Dosha assessment (name, email, phone)
  • Complete our Prakriti Quiz (responses and optional email)
  • Communicate with us via email or WhatsApp

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain technical data including:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type, version, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent, and referring URLs
  • Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)

Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from Google Analytics (aggregated usage data) and Google Tag Manager. We use Firebase (Google) to store form submissions securely.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide services: responding to your enquiries, scheduling consultations, and delivering sessions
  • To communicate: sending session confirmations, follow-up care notes, and programme information
  • To send newsletters: only if you have explicitly subscribed; you can unsubscribe at any time
  • To improve our website: analysing how visitors use our site to improve content and user experience
  • To personalise recommendations: using your Prakriti Quiz results to suggest relevant programmes
  • To comply with legal obligations: retaining records as required by applicable Indian law

We do not use your information for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: trusted third-party tools we use to operate our business (Google Firebase for data storage, Google Analytics for analytics, Zoom for session delivery). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Legal requirements: if required by law, court order, or government authority in India
  • Business transfers: in the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred as part of business assets; you will be notified in advance
  • With your consent: in any other circumstance, only with your explicit written consent

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate and improve our website. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: required for basic website functionality (e.g., remembering your cookie preferences)
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics 4 (GA4) — used to understand how visitors interact with our site in aggregated, anonymised form
  • Preference cookies: remembering your dark/light mode preference

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Declining analytics cookies will not affect your ability to use our services. You can also opt out of Google Analytics at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against accidental loss, unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These include:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted through our website
  • Firebase Security Rules restricting database access to authenticated requests only
  • Regular security reviews and updates
  • Limited internal access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: request that we limit how we process your data
  • Portability: request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing
  • Withdraw consent: where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@thesamarpan.co.in. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at support@thesamarpan.co.in and we will take steps to delete such information.

9. International Data Transfers

Samarpan is based in New Delhi, India. We serve clients globally. When you submit information through our website, that data is processed and stored on Google Firebase servers, which may be located outside your country of residence.

By using our Services, you consent to the transfer of your information to India and to servers operated by our third-party service providers internationally. We ensure that any such transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of our Services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
