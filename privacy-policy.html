Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Samarpan ("we", "us", or "our"), operated by Harsh Ranjan, collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you visit thesamarpan.co.in or engage with our services.

By using our website you agree to the practices described here. Please read this carefully before proceeding.

01

02

Information We Collect

We collect information in three ways: directly from you, automatically through your use of the site, and occasionally from third-party services.

A. Information You Provide Directly
  • Contact & Booking Forms: Name, email address, phone number, and health concern details when you fill out our consultation or contact forms.
  • Newsletter Subscription: Email address (and optional name) when you subscribe to our Inner Circle newsletter.
  • Session Information: Health history, dosha-related details, and wellness goals shared during consultations. This is sensitive health data handled with the highest care and confidentiality.
  • Community Applications: Name, credentials, area of practice, and professional background when applying to join the Samarpan Community.
  • Communications: Messages, emails, or WhatsApp conversations you initiate with us.
  • Prakriti Quiz: Responses to our on-site Dosha quiz if you choose to share your name and email for your personalised report.
B. Information Collected Automatically
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, referring URLs, and navigation patterns via Google Analytics (GA4).
  • Device & Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and language settings.
  • Browser Storage: Dark/light mode preference stored locally in your browser — not transmitted to any server.
  • Session Storage: Popup display history (to prevent repetitive popups) stored locally per browser session only.
C. Information from Third Parties
  • Google Analytics: Aggregated, anonymised website usage statistics.
  • Firebase / Firestore: Form submission data stored securely in Google's Firebase cloud infrastructure.
  • Social Media: If you interact with us on Instagram or YouTube, those platforms may share basic engagement data per their own privacy policies.

04

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information under the following legal bases, consistent with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India) and, where applicable, GDPR principles for our international clients:

  • Consent: For newsletter subscriptions and non-essential cookies. You may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Contractual Necessity: For processing enquiries and delivering consultation services you have requested.
  • Legitimate Interests: For website analytics, security monitoring, and improving our services — where these interests do not override your fundamental rights.
  • Legal Obligation: Where required to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or court orders.
05

Sharing & Disclosure

We share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Google Firebase (database), Google Analytics (analytics), and Google Workspace (email). These providers operate under data processing agreements and are prohibited from using your data for their own purposes.
  • WhatsApp (Meta): When you choose to contact us via WhatsApp, your messages are processed by Meta under their own privacy policy.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority, we may disclose your information to the extent legally required and will notify you where permitted.
  • Business Transfer: In the unlikely event of a business sale or merger, personal data may be transferred to the acquiring party subject to equivalent privacy protections.

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with marketers, advertisers, or data brokers.

07

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Consultation & Session Records: Retained for a minimum of 5 years after your last session, as required for health records and potential follow-up care.
  • Newsletter Subscriptions: Retained until you unsubscribe. You may unsubscribe at any time via the link in any newsletter or by contacting us directly.
  • General Enquiries & Form Submissions: Retained for 2 years from the date of last contact.
  • Analytics Data: Google Analytics retains anonymised data per their own retention settings (typically 14 months).
  • Community Applications: Retained for 1 year if not accepted; indefinitely for accepted members as part of the community directory.
  • Quiz Results: Retained for 1 year for service improvement purposes; anonymised thereafter.

When data is no longer required, we securely delete or irreversibly anonymise it.

08

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have some or all of the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Ask us to limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is consent-based, withdraw it at any time.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@thesamarpan.co.in. We will respond within 30 days and may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

We treat every privacy request with the same care and intention as our healing work. Your wellbeing — including your data privacy — matters deeply to us.

09

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction:

  • HTTPS encryption across all website pages and form submissions.
  • Google Firebase Firestore with security rules restricting access to authorised systems only.
  • Limited internal access to personal data on a strict need-to-know basis.
  • Regular review of our data handling practices and third-party service configurations.

While we take all reasonable precautions, no system is 100% secure. If you become aware of any security concern related to our website, please contact us immediately at info@thesamarpan.co.in.

10

Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will promptly delete such data.

Wellness consultations for minors are conducted only with the explicit written consent of a parent or guardian who must remain present throughout the session.

11

Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to external platforms including Instagram, YouTube, and WhatsApp. These are provided for your convenience. We have no control over the content or privacy practices of these third-party sites and are not responsible for their policies.

Embedded resources such as Google Fonts, Tailwind CDN, and Lucide icons may result in your browser making requests to third-party servers. This is standard for modern websites and typically involves only minimal data exchange (your IP address and browser type).

12

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
  • Notify newsletter subscribers via email for material changes.
  • Post a notice on the website homepage for a reasonable period.

Your continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
