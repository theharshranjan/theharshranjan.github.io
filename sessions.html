❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our sessions

Is this medical treatment or therapy?

No, these sessions are educational and wellness-oriented. They complement but don't replace medical care or licensed psychotherapy. Always consult qualified healthcare professionals for medical concerns or mental health diagnosis.

Do I need prior experience in Yoga or Ayurveda?

Absolutely not! All sessions are tailored to your current level. Whether you're a complete beginner or experienced practitioner, we'll meet you where you are and build from there with patience and clarity.

How does the donation-based model work?

Pay what feels right and accessible for you after experiencing the session. There's no fixed price—your contribution supports the continuation of this work. UPI payment details are provided after booking.

Can I get written notes or a practice routine?

Yes! After each session, you'll receive a personalized summary with key insights, practice recommendations, and actionable steps you can integrate into your daily life.

What if I'm in a different time zone?

No problem! While standard hours are 6:00–8:00 PM IST, flexible scheduling is available for international clients. We'll find a time that works for both of us.

How do I prepare for my first session?

Simply come as you are with an open mind. For yoga sessions, wear comfortable clothing and have a quiet space. For all sessions, ensure a stable internet connection and minimal distractions.
