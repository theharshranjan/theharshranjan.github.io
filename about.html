Our Story

Born from a Deep
Desire to Serve

"Samarpan means total surrender."

Samarpan was born not from strategy, but from a calling. A calling to bridge the 5,000-year-old wisdom of Vedic sciences with the lived reality of modern human suffering — anxiety, disconnection, chronic illness, and the quiet ache of a life unlived.

What began as a personal practice became a path of service. Today, Samarpan holds space for seekers across 13+ countries — professionals, parents, students, and pilgrims — all arriving with the same silent question: How do I come home to myself?

We answer that question not with a formula, but with a relationship. Every protocol is handcrafted. Every session is a conversation. And every healing journey is honoured as sacred.

Our Mission

Make holistic healing accessible, personalised, and transformative.

Our Vision

A world where every human being knows their own nature and lives in harmony with it.

Philosophy

Rooted in Living Tradition

Samarpan's teachings flow from an unbroken lineage of yogic wisdom — transmitted not through books alone, but through direct experience and devoted practice.

The Inner Science

Yoga as a Complete Science

The tradition we carry forward is not merely physical. It is a comprehensive system that works simultaneously on the body, the breath, the mind, and consciousness — a living science passed from teacher to student across generations.

The Teaching

Wisdom Lived, Not Merely Spoken

Our practices — including Ajapa Japa, Yoga Nidra, and mantra — are ancient tantric and yogic methods, transmitted through direct initiation and personal guidance. The lineage breathes through the practice itself.

Sacred Practices

Integrated with Modern Understanding

We honour the integrity of these ancient teachings while making them intelligible to the modern seeker — integrating neuroscience, psychology, and Ayurveda to offer a truly whole-person approach.

"We do not teach yoga. We live it — and invite others to do the same."

Founder & Lead Practitioner
Founder & Lead Practitioner

Harsh Ranjan

Yoga · Ayurveda · Psychology · Mantra

Harsh Ranjan is the founder of Samarpan and an integrative wellness practitioner with over six years of dedicated immersion in the Vedic sciences. His journey began in the ashrams of India — living, breathing, and absorbing the teachings of yoga, Ayurveda, and meditation not as subjects of study, but as a way of life.

Trained in classical Hatha Yoga, Pranayama, Yoga Nidra, Ajapa Japa, Ayurvedic constitution analysis, and depth psychology, Harsh brings a rare integration of inner science with practical healing tools. His sessions are characterised by deep listening, precision, and an ability to meet each person exactly where they are.

Today, he guides clients from 13+ countries — online and in sacred retreat spaces across India — offering a bridge between ancient wisdom and the modern human experience.

Lella Naga
Durga Bhavani

Ayurveda · BAMS · Sareer Kriya Gold Medallist

Dr. Lella Naga Durga Bhavani is a Founding Member & in final-year BAMS (Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery) doctor at Dr. Noori Ramasastry Ayurvedic Medical College, Andhra Pradesh — and a Gold Medallist in Sareer Kriya (Ayurvedic Physiology), the most foundational science of the human body in classical Ayurveda.

Her academic distinction reflects not just intellectual mastery, but a deep and sincere devotion to the classical texts — the Charaka Samhita, Sushruta Samhita, and Ashtanga Hridayam — which she approaches with the reverence of a true student of the tradition.

At Samarpan, Leela contributes her rigorous classical Ayurvedic knowledge through the Wisdom Stream Journal, community outreach, and research collaboration — bridging the living tradition of the Vaidyas with the modern wellness seeker.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Sacred Service

We approach every healing session as an act of service, not a transaction. The seeker's wellbeing is always the highest priority.

Authentic Wisdom

We offer only what we have lived and practised ourselves. No borrowed philosophy — only embodied, tested, transmitted knowledge.

Wholeness

True healing addresses the body, the breath, the emotions, and consciousness. We refuse to fragment what is inherently whole.

Global Accessibility

Ancient wisdom belongs to all of humanity. Our online presence ensures geography is never a barrier to healing.

Deep Integrity

Confidentiality, respect, and honesty are non-negotiable. We hold every story shared with us as a sacred trust.

Continuous Sadhana

Our practitioners continue their own practice and learning always. We teach from the depth of ongoing personal transformation.
