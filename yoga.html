"Yoga is not about achieving the perfect posture.

Research Corner

Where Practice Meets Evidence

Yoga research, interpreted through a Samarpan lens

Mental Health

Yoga & Depression

Study

Trials of structured Hatha Yoga programmes report meaningful reductions in depressive symptom scores.

Samarpan view

Asana and Pranayama are framed as tools that steady Prana Vayu, the subtle air governing mood and mind.

Takeaway

Best combined with counselling rather than used as a stand-alone treatment.

Metabolic Health

Yoga & Diabetes

Study

Regular practice is associated with improved fasting glucose and better insulin sensitivity in several studies.

Samarpan view

Specific asana sequences are chosen to stimulate the pancreas and support healthy Agni.

Takeaway

Practice alongside prescribed medication and diet, never as a replacement.

Cardiovascular Health

Yoga & Hypertension

Study

Meta-analyses link consistent practice with modest but reliable reductions in systolic and diastolic pressure.

Samarpan view

Slow, even Pranayama is used specifically to calm an aggravated Pitta-Vata state.

Takeaway

Ten minutes of slow breathing daily is a low-risk addition to a cardiac care plan.
