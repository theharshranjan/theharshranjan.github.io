My Approach

I offer integrative, psycho-educational counseling and coaching. Sessions are conversational and practical, drawing on:

  • Evidence-informed cognitive & behavioral psychology
  • Meditation practices (Yoga Nidra, mindfulness, breathwork)
  • Yogic principles (yamas, niyamas, pranayama)
  • Ayurvedic lifestyle rhythms and self-care

Note: BA (Hons) Psychology student (IGNOU). I provide wellness-focused guidance and do not diagnose or treat mental disorders.

Meditation Integration

Simple, practical techniques that fit seamlessly into your daily life

Yoga Nidra (10–20 mins)

Guided rest for nervous system regulation and improved sleep.

  • Reduce mental rumination & physical tension
  • Enhance recovery and cognitive function

Breath-Led Focus (3–5 mins)

Quick anchoring practices for anytime use:

  • Box Breathing: 4-4-4-4 for calm focus
  • Extended Exhale: 4-6 for relaxation
  • Anulom-Vilom: Alternate nostril for balance

Mindful Check-Ins (1–2 mins)

Micro-pauses to scan posture, breath, and intention before tasks.

  • Build present-moment awareness
  • Reduce reactive patterns

Grounding & Defusion

ACT-style cognitive defusion and 5-4-3-2-1 grounding method.

  • Create space between thoughts and reactions
  • Cultivate emotional flexibility

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our counseling sessions

Is this medical or psychiatric treatment?

No. This is educational, wellness-focused counseling. It is not a substitute for licensed medical or psychiatric care. If experiencing severe mental health challenges, consult with a licensed mental health professional.

Can I attend sessions from outside India?

Absolutely! Sessions are conducted online and open worldwide. We'll coordinate time zones to find a suitable slot that works for both of us.

What techniques and tools might we use?

Our work may include journaling prompts, cognitive reframing, breathwork, mindfulness exercises, habit scaffolding, lifestyle alignment with Ayurvedic principles, and personalized meditation practices.

How is my privacy protected?

Your information is handled with strict confidentiality within the limits of the law and the secure platforms used for scheduling and video calls. I respect your privacy and create a safe, judgment-free space.

What if I'm experiencing a mental health crisis?

If in immediate danger, contact emergency services right away. In India, dial 112 or call KIRAN: 1800-599-0019 (24×7 mental health support). Outside India, contact your local emergency services.

How many sessions will I need?

This varies by individual and goals. Some clients find value in a single session for specific guidance, while others prefer ongoing support. We can discuss what feels right during our first session.

⚠️ Important Crisis Resources: If in immediate danger, dial 112 in India. For 24×7 mental health support, call KIRAN: 1800-599-0019. Outside India, contact your local emergency services. This service offers wellness guidance and does not replace professional mental health treatment.
