The Journey

Rooted in Classical Ayurveda,
Rising Through Excellence

Lella Naga Durga Bhavani's path into Ayurveda was not chosen lightly — it was a calling grounded in reverence for classical Indian medicine. From her earliest days at Dr. Noori Ramasastry Ayurvedic Medical College, she demonstrated both academic brilliance and an uncommon depth of engagement with the ancient texts.

Consistently placing first across three consecutive academic years, she has not merely studied Ayurveda — she has lived it. Her article "Quantum Physics in Ayurveda" reflects a mind that bridges millennia, finding resonance between Vedic cosmology and modern scientific inquiry.

Beyond the classroom, she has carried this knowledge into communities — conducting Ayurvedic awareness programmes in schools, participating in medical camps, and representing the oral tradition at state-level Sloka recitation competitions as a three-time consecutive state topper.
